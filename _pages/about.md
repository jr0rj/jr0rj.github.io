I am a PhD student in Uncertainty Quantification currently working on machine learning for Bayesian Inverse problems. I am under the supervision of Professor Simon Cotter and Professor Thomas House at the University of Manchester.

My areas of mathematical interest include: uncertainty quantification, optimisation, Markov Chain Monte Carlo and statistics for health sciences.
