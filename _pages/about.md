I am a PhD student in Uncertainty Quantification currently working on machine learning for Bayesian Inverse problems. I am under the supervision of [Professor Simon Cotter](https://personalpages.manchester.ac.uk/staff/simon.cotter/index.html) and [Professor Thomas House](https://personalpages.manchester.ac.uk/staff/thomas.house/blog/index-1.html) at the University of Manchester.

My areas of mathematical interest include: uncertainty quantification, optimisation, Markov Chain Monte Carlo and statistics for health sciences.
