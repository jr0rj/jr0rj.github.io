I am a PhD student in Uncertainty Quantification currently working on machine learning for Bayesian Inverse problems. I am under the supervision of <a href="https://personalpages.manchester.ac.uk/staff/simon.cotter/index.html" target="_blank">Professor Simon Cotter</a> and <a href="https://personalpages.manchester.ac.uk/staff/thomas.house/about.html" target="_blank">Professor Thomas House</a> at the University of Manchester.

My areas of mathematical interest include: uncertainty quantification, optimisation, Markov Chain Monte Carlo and statistics for health sciences.
